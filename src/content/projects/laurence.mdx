Laurence is a real-time bid optimizer for Amazon PPC. It treats every keyword
as a Bayesian decision problem, pools evidence across a portfolio → ASIN →
cluster → keyword hierarchy, and sets bids under an explicit CVaR risk
budget. Built for brands spending serious money on Amazon; currently running
on tens of millions of ad spend.
