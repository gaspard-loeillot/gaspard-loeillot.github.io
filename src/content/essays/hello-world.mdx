I'm starting this site for one reason: to think out loud in a place that's mine.

For most of my career my ideas have lived inside Notion documents, Slack
threads, and late-night voice memos. Some of them deserve more. Writing in
public is the forcing function I've been avoiding — and the one I most need.

The plan is simple:

- **Essays** — finished thoughts, published when they'd survive a re-read.
- **Podcasts** — conversations with people I learn from.
- **Projects** — things I've shipped or am shipping now.
- **Quotes** — lines that bent my thinking.

No analytics chasing, no SEO performance art. Just a working notebook I'm
willing to show people.

If you end up here, thanks for reading.
